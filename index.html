About Me

People first.
Systems second.

I got into healthcare by accident and stayed because I genuinely enjoy helping people navigate hard moments.

Areas of experience
Healthcare Operations Behavioral Health Addiction Recovery Enrollment Management Team Leadership Process & SOP Design AI & Automation Customer Success Content Creation Website Building

I started in recovery work — managing a sober living facility, helping residents rebuild their lives after addiction. That experience shaped everything about how I operate: I lead with empathy, I take accountability seriously, and I genuinely care about the people I work with and for.

These days I'm running enrollment operations in pediatric behavioral health, building the workflows that connect families to care. I love the problem-solving side of it — there's always something broken that can be made better, and I can't leave a busted process alone until it runs the way it should.

Outside of work I keep it pretty simple — working out at the gym, walking my dog Casper, and enjoying good food around Dallas. I've been deep in the AI rabbit hole for the past couple years and it's changed how I work completely — I use it to move faster, cut the busywork, and focus on the stuff that actually matters. I also love teaching it to others, and I'm always happy to show someone how to actually use these tools day-to-day.
